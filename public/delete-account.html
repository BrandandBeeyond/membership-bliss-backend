Delete Your Account

Last Updated: 19 June 2026

This page explains how users of the Touchwood Bliss Membership App can request deletion of their account and personal data.

To request account deletion, please email us from your registered email address or include your registered mobile number so we can verify your identity before processing the request.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to reservations@touchwoodbliss.com with the subject line: "Delete My Touchwood Bliss Account"
  2. Include your registered mobile number and registered email address.
  3. Our team will verify your request and process eligible account deletion within 7 to 30 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile information
  • Your membership account details
  • Your saved personal information
  • Your saved addresses
  • Your authentication and login information
  • Personal information directly associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained when required for legal, accounting, taxation, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, payment verification, regulatory compliance, or business record purposes.

This may include:

  • Membership purchase records
  • Invoices and payment references
  • Transaction history
  • Booking and reservation records
  • Customer support communications
  • Information required under applicable laws
